The Bollywood film industry, centered in Mumbai India, is renowned for its prolific output of vibrant and colorful films
that blend drama, romance, comedy, and music. With roots dating back to the early 20th century, Bollywood has evolved into a global phenomenon, producing a diverse range of movies annually, from big-budget blockbusters to independent productions.
Its stars, including Shah Rukh Khan, Amitabh Bachchan, and Deepika Padukone, enjoy immense popularity both in India and internationally
